About the publication

Useful work before an introduction.

The Product Dossier studies products deeply enough to offer their builders something worth challenging.

Creator and researcher

Rohit Singh

I created The Product Dossier to replace generic outreach with independent, evidence-first product work. The aim is not to pretend to know a company from the outside. It is to map what is publicly knowable, expose what is uncertain and propose experiments that could be useful to the people building the product.

Edition 001 investigates Delta Exchange India through official documentation, legal and technical material, public product surfaces, a statistical analysis of 22,199 app reviews and a separate adversarial citation audit.

01

Evidence before opinion

Major claims link to a source, a dataset or an explicit synthesis.

02

Respect the inside view

Public research cannot replace internal telemetry, operational context or the judgment of the product team.

03

Make uncertainty visible

Counter-evidence, limitations and rejected claims remain part of the published work.

04

Recommend experiments

Ideas are framed as bounded tests with evidence requirements—not confident prescriptions.

Independent publication. The Product Dossier is not affiliated with, commissioned by or endorsed by Delta Exchange. It does not provide trading, investment, legal, tax or security advice.
